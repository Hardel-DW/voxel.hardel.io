# Overview
Version 5.8.0 of NeoEnchant brings significant improvements to performance, enchantment rebalancing and the addition of multilingual support.

---

# Multilingual Support
This version brings 5 new languages, generated by **Claude-3.5-Sonnet-2024-10-22** from the French and English translations of version 2.0.0.
These translations serve as a base and can be improved by the community via our Discord.
The Japanese language is made by a community member.

### New Languages
- German
- Spanish
- Japanese
- Russian
- Chinese

### Contribution
- Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/TAmVFvkHep) to follow updates and contribute to translation
- You can also contribute to translation by making a ticket on Github.

# Compatibility and Support
- Full support for Minecraft 1.21 - 1.21.4
- Compatibility with Yggdrasil 3.0.0
- Support for latest versions of BeyondEnchant
