# Overview
Version 5.8.0 of NeoEnchant brings significant improvements to performance, enchantment rebalancing and the addition of multilingual support.

# Reworked Enchantments

:::enchant.changelog{name="Rebound (Stable)"}
After a long period of development, Rebound finally exits its experimental phase with major improvements to stability and gameplay.

::enchant.newenchant{icon="/images/features/item/bow.webp", max_level=3, anvil_cost=2, rarity=4}

### Rebounds
Arrows now bounce off walls, previously they only bounced between monsters.

### Effects
Effects now trigger on each bounce
:::

:::enchant.changelog{name="Poison Aspect"}
Fixed a bug that affected the Poison Aspect enchantment, the poison effect triggered on non-players.

### Impact effects
Now affects players with poison effect

### Balance
Before, poison effect and wither effect triggered on non-players, now only poison effect will be active on monsters. And poison on players will be reduced.
:::

:::enchant.changelog{name="Pull"}
A bug prevented the Pull enchantment from working, it is now fixed.

### Reload
Now gives eggs when you deal damage to a monster
:::

:::enchant.changelog{name="VeinMiner & MiningPlus"}
Improved gameplay with more precise activation

### Activation
This effect now triggers only in crouching position
:::

:::enchant.changelog{name="Fury"}
Improved enchantment power

### Damage
Increased damage dealt
:::

:::enchant.changelog{name="Sky Walk"}
Adjustment for better control

### Mobility
Reduced propulsion for better control
:::

:::enchant.changelog{name="Storm Arrow"}
Rebalancing maximum power

### Maximum Level
Maximum level reduced from 2 to 1
:::

:::enchant.changelog{name="Builder Arms"}
Improved construction range

### Range
Range increased from 1.5 to 2 blocks per level
:::

:::enchant.changelog{name="Armored"}
Enhanced protection

### Protection
Protection has been strengthened for better defense
:::

:::enchant.changelog{name="Dwarfed & Oversize"}
Rebalancing negative effects

### Balance
Negative effects have been reduced, it was too violent
:::

# Multilingual Support
This version brings 5 new languages, generated by **Claude-3.5-Sonnet-2024-10-22** from the French and English translations of version 2.0.0.
These translations serve as a base and can be improved by the community via our Discord.
The Japanese language is made by a community member.

### New Languages
- German
- Spanish
- Japanese
- Russian
- Chinese

### Contribution
- Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/TAmVFvkHep) to follow updates and contribute to translation
- You can also contribute to translation by making a ticket on Github.

# Compatibility and Support
- Full support for Minecraft 1.21 - 1.21.4
- Compatibility with Yggdrasil 3.0.0
- Support for latest versions of BeyondEnchant
