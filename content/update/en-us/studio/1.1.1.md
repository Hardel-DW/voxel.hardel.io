## Small Fixes for 1.1.1

Some small fixes after the new **Mode** feature.

- The "Non Combinable" enchantments list was deleted in "Creative Only" mode.
- Some features were deleted in "Creative Only" mode like
  "Prevent/Curse/Smelting".
- Fixed a bug that crashed the application when trying to access the
  enchantments incompatibilities.
