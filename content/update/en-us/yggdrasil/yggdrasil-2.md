# Overview
The new version of Yggdrasil pack 2.0.0 adds two new structures, the Asflors village and the Runic Fracture, and introduces "The Dark Alfheim" with significant reworks. Combat difficulty has been revised as well as the loot system and many new mechanics have been added.

## How to get translations
- You must put the **NeoEnchant** pack in your resource pack folder.
- Alternatively, you can use the **Voxel Translation** resource pack, which includes translations for all **Voxel** content (NeoEnchant, Yggdrasil, and future packs).

# Asflors Villages
Discover the peaceful **Asflors Villages**, a peaceful community. These colonies living in flourishing places, possess a rich, sad and moving history. Immerse yourself in the history of Yggdrasil and discover the story of its first character, lost in the secrets of the village.

### Epic quest and legendary weapon
Embark on an epic quest that tests intelligence and perseverance. As you progress, unlock exclusive and unique enchantments. A weapon is hidden, the **Asflors Sword**, obtainable only after completing a long and difficult series of tasks.

### Rewards and secrets
- **Exclusive enchantments**: Access two powerful enchantments never seen before.
- **Legendary Asflors sword**: Discover the ultimate prize, a weapon of unmatched power.
- **Rich lore**: Immerse yourself in the deep and moving story of an ancient character.
- **Hidden treasures**: Carefully explore the village, and discover many scattered secrets.

# Runic Fracture
Explore the enigmatic **Runic Fracture**, a compact structure intrinsically linked to Asflors Villages. This area is locked by a padlock and can only be accessed with specific keys.

![Runic Fracture](/images/background/blog/runic_fracture.jpg)

# Alfheim
The Dark Alfheim, this alternative version is accessible only with dark potions, it introduces a new level of challenge. The monsters in this realm use superior armor and enchantments. The creatures here have developed immunities to fire, knockback and fall damage.

New monsters and keys appear in this dark version, explore the places well, I wish you a good game.

![Alfheim](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/59cfeed443538125c4c40af5bba64ef2bb282c83.png)

### Alfheim Comparison

|  | Normal Version | Dark Version |
|---------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Difficulty** | Reduced | High |
| **Monsters** | Standard | More numerous and powerful |
| **Normal keys** | Easy to find | Easy to find |
| **Dark keys** | Very rare | Frequent |
| **Rewards** | Normal | Improved |
| **Dark potions** | Findable | Required |
| **Monster armor** | None | Enchanted |
| **Exclusive enchantments** | Common and Rare | Rare and Unique |
| **Effects on monsters** | None | Resistant to fire, knockback and fall damage |
