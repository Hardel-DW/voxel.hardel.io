# Overview
Version 3.0.0 of the Yggdrasil pack marks a major milestone with the introduction of a brand new dimension: the Runic Realm. This update also brings a significant expansion of Asflors Villages, new epic structures, and a complete overhaul of Alfheim.

# The Runic Realm
Discover a mysterious and empty new dimension, left by the dark elves who escaped their traumas, hunted by the gods, this dimension is a forgotten world, empty designed for revenge.

### Access and Exploration
- **Runic Island Portal**: A new portal located on the Runic Island allows access to this mysterious dimension
- **Ancient Civilizations**: Discover the remains of forgotten civilizations
- **Structures**: Labyrinths, ruins, villages and much more
- **Rewards**: Legendary items, unique enchantments, legendary weapons

![Runic Realm](/images/background/blog/yggdrasil/runic_dimensions.webp)

# New Structures

## The Runic Labyrinth
The second mega structure of Yggdrasil spanning 500x500 blocks and rising 150 blocks in height. This complex maze contains:
- Unique legendary weapons
- Particularly precious loot
- Unique items like Ran's apples
- Its own story to discover
- A more than difficult challenge

# Major Improvements

### Asflors Villages Extension
The Asflors village has been expanded, with a new series of books continuing the quest, leading you to the new dimension of the dark elves.

- New series of books continuing the quest
- The altar containing the main quest book now generates in all villages
- Enriched narrative content

### Alfheim Overhaul
- Difficulty rebalancing
- Revision of the reward system, shulkers are replaced by chests and the following loot is removed:
  - Shulker-related content
  - Nether Star
  - Netherite-related content

# Gameplay Balance
This update brings significant adjustments for a more immersive experience:
- Overall difficulty rebalancing
- Revision of encounters in structures
