# Overview
NeoEnchant version 5.8.0 brings significant performance improvements, enchantment rebalancing, and adds multilingual support.

## How to Get Translations
- Place the **NeoEnchant** pack in your resource packs folder.
- Alternatively, you can use the **Voxel Translation** pack, which includes translations for all **Voxel** content (NeoEnchant, Yggdrasil, and future packs).

# Multilingual Support
This version adds 5 new languages, generated by **Claude-3.5-Sonnet-2024-10-22** from the French and English translations of version 2.0.0.
These translations serve as a foundation and can be improved by the community through our Discord.

### New Languages
- German
- Spanish
- Japanese
- Russian
- Chinese

### Contribution
- Join our Discord to participate in improving translations
- Report translation errors through our ticket system
- Propose your own translations for other languages

# Compatibility and Support
- Full support for Minecraft 1.21 - 1.21.4
- Improved compatibility with Yggdrasil 3.0.0
- Support for the latest versions of BeyondEnchant
