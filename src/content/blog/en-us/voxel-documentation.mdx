# Interactive Documentation for Yggdrasil
We are excited to present our new interactive documentation for Yggdrasil, designed to make your gaming experience more transparent and intuitive.

## Structure Exploration
The new documentation allows you to **View each structure in detail** and **Browse available loot block by block**.

## Block-by-Block Loot System
Our new documentation system enables precise exploration:
- Click on any reward block (Shulker, Chest, Trial Spawner, Vault, Pottery) to see its contents
- View drop probabilities for each item **(Soon)**
- Discover rare items and their spawn conditions
- Explore different loot variants based on difficulty
