Version 3.0.0 of the Yggdrasil pack marks a major milestone with the introduction of a brand new dimension: the Runic Realm. This update also brings a significant expansion of the Asflors Villages, epic new structures, and a complete overhaul of Alfheim.

# The Runic Realm
Discover a mysterious empty dimension, left behind by the dark elves who escaped their traumas, hunted by the gods, this dimension is a forgotten world, empty and designed for vengeance.

### Access and Exploration
- **Runic Island Portal**: A new portal located on the Runic Island allows access to this mysterious dimension
- **Ancient Civilizations**: Discover the remnants of forgotten civilizations
- **Structures**: Labyrinths, ruins, villages, and much more
- **Rewards**: Legendary items, unique enchantments, legendary weapons

# New Structures

## The Runic Labyrinth
Yggdrasil's second mega-structure spanning 500x500 blocks and rising 150 blocks high. This complex maze contains:
- Unique legendary weapons
- Particularly valuable loot
- Unique items like Ran's apples
- Its own story to discover
- More than challenging difficulty

# Major Improvements

### Asflors Villages Extension
The Asflors village has been expanded, with a new series of books continuing the quest, leading you to the new dark elves dimension.

- New series of books continuing the quest
- The altar containing the main quest book now generates in all villages
- Enriched narrative content

### Alfheim Overhaul
- Difficulty rebalancing
- Revision of the reward system, shulkers are replaced by chests and the following loot has been removed:
  - Shulker-related content
  - Nether Star
  - Netherite-related content
